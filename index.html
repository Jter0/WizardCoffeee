Welcome to Wizard

Discover the perfect blend of taste and ambiance at Wizard. Our passion for quality coffee and exceptional service is reflected in every cup we serve. Whether you're here for a quick pick-me-up or a relaxing break, Wizard is your ideal destination.

Menu

Explore our carefully crafted menu, designed to satisfy your coffee cravings and more.

Coffee

  • Espresso - A rich, full-bodied shot of pure coffee perfection.
  • Americano - Espresso diluted with hot water, offering a smooth, robust flavor.
  • Cappuccino - A harmonious blend of espresso, steamed milk, and a delicate layer of foam.
  • Latte - Espresso mixed with generous steamed milk and topped with a light foam.
  • Mocha - A delightful combination of espresso, chocolate, and steamed milk, topped with whipped cream.

Signature Drinks

  • Wizard Special - Our exclusive blend of espresso, caramel, and a touch of vanilla, finished with steamed milk.
  • Iced Wizard Delight - Refreshing iced coffee with a hint of mint and a splash of cream.

Cold Brews

  • Classic Cold Brew - Slowly steeped to perfection, offering a smooth and bold taste.
  • Vanilla Sweet Cream Cold Brew - Cold brew topped with our house-made vanilla sweet cream.

Non-Coffee Beverages

  • Matcha Latte - Premium matcha green tea blended with steamed milk for a creamy, refreshing drink.
  • Chai Tea Latte - A spicy and sweet mix of chai tea and steamed milk, topped with a sprinkle of cinnamon.
  • Hot Chocolate - Rich and creamy, made with high-quality cocoa and steamed milk.

Bakery

  • Croissants - Freshly baked, buttery, and flaky, perfect for any time of day.
  • Muffins - A variety of flavors including blueberry, chocolate chip, and bran, baked fresh daily.
  • Pastries - A selection of delectable pastries, including Danish, tarts, and scones.

Sandwiches and Salads

  • Chicken Caesar Wrap - Grilled chicken, crisp romaine, and Caesar dressing, all wrapped in a soft tortilla.
  • Veggie Panini - Grilled vegetables, mozzarella, and pesto on artisan bread.
  • Quinoa Salad - A healthy mix of quinoa, fresh vegetables, and a light vinaigrette.

Desserts

  • Cheesecake - Creamy and smooth, with a graham cracker crust, available in various flavors.
  • Brownies - Rich and fudgy, perfect for chocolate lovers.
  • Fruit Tart - A buttery crust filled with creamy custard and topped with fresh, seasonal fruits.
